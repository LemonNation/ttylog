Version v0.1.b was first released in January of 2002 by the original authur, Tibor Koleszar, as ttylog v0.1.b-1 and v0.1.b-2. Another release of it was done by him: v0.1.b-3 in November of 2002. Andrew Pollock of the Debian QA group then released v0.1.b-4 in April of 2006.
